I am Mansa the DESIGNER

I love: ABCDE: Architecture, Biomimicry, Culture, Design, and Ergonomics!

I love learning about: how buildings and spaces affect people, how people use spaces in their own ways, how culture changes places, and the stories behind how a city is built.

My burning question: How do we design better workspaces for everyone?

What I Create: Posters, flashcards, storybooks, workbooks, Teaching Learning Materials

My Learning Worlds: Bloom, Design, Craft

Learning Style: I sketch first and build second. I love to plan. I also break big tasks into small steps. I also learn by organising.

Favourite catchphrase: I have an idea!

Favourite Drinks & Foods: Atadwe milk, ewokple with fetri detsi, toto beef jollof, coconut cake, banana pancakes
